About TaskManager Application
TaskManager is the official mobile application IA IEU on Android Platform. It is specifically designed for IA IEU only. Using TaskManager we can track and update our field force daily activities. If you are not part of Electronic Governance Infrastructure Executive Agency authorized personnel this application is not meant for you.
